The Pain

 For centuries, women have been put in boxes of how we should look, how we should express ourselves, and in which way we have to contribute to this world. In this masculine world the pressure and false expectations from society, loved ones, and ourselves leaves us feeling disconnected from our bodies, our sexiulaity, our pleasure and our true power and purpose. Meet your Coach

 After hosting being part of 50+ personal growth programs at events like Mindvalley University, A-fest, Bali Spirit Festival, Tantra Festivals, you name it and helping over 200+ women reclaim their right to be exactly who they are... It’s still hard to believe where it all started. Growing up in a traditional household in Armenia, I felt completely disconnected with my sexuality, my body, and my femininity. I was lost in what’s right and wrong, I felt suffocated in that cage they’ve been taming me for 26 years. I knew I am a free spirited woman, but I didn't know how to BE it, didn;t have tools to reconnect to her. So I quit my 9-6 banking career and went on a journey. After years of soul-searching and deep inner work, travelling through 40+ countries and being in countless ashrams, meeting coaches, mentors, I felt like I arrived home, back to my body, my wisdom, my pleasure and intuition as my compass. I remembered that sacred feminine in me at tantra festivals, released so much shame and guilt in osho festivals, NLP certifications, danced with my spirit in BaliSpirit Festivals, met my inner buddha at Vipassana and yoga teacher training in India, Bali, Thailand. After years of experiential learning, I founded the She Circles woman circle at Mindvalley to give women a space to express themselves fully with no filter. And now I’ve taken all of this knowledge and wrapped it up into a powerful coaching program to help women reignite that fiery feminine power inside and remember how valuable they truly are.
